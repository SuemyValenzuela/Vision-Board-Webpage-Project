🔥VISUALIZATION OF MY FEELINGS🔥

  1. "Never dull your shine for somebody else." – Tyra Banks: This quote for me means that you shouldn't change who you are because of others; you must shine and be confident in all you do, regardless of what others think. People that truly care about you will be there to support you.
  2. “Family and friends are hidden treasures, seek them and enjoy their riches.” – Wanda Hope Carter: In our life, we have family and friends. We must take care of them because if we lose them, we would be left with nothing; thus, we must value them beyond everything else.
  3. “Love yourself unconditionally, just as you love those closest to you despite their faults.” —Les Brown: This is significant because we love people with all of their imperfections, but we are often incapable of loving ourselves with all of our defects. That is why, just as we give love to others, we must also give love to ourselves and accept ourselves for who we are.
